Who We Are

Whiskers Pethouse is a trusted pet care provider and pet care shop who dedicated to the health, happiness, and well-being of your beloved pets. Founded with a passion for animals, we offer a full range of services from daycare, grooming, veterinary consultations, to training programs. Our team consists of certified professionals committed to providing compassionate care for every pet.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure that every pet receives the love, care, and attention they deserve. We aim to provide a safe and enjoyable environment for pets while offering peace of mind to their owners. Through continuous learning and collaboration, Whiskers Pethouse strives to lead in pet care excellence.

Our Vision

To become the leading pet care provider in the region, known for innovation, compassion, and excellence in pet services. We envision a world where pets and their owners enjoy healthier, happier, and more fulfilling lives together.
